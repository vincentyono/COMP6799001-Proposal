Problem Statement

Gaming is a huge industry nowadays and companies have made billions in profit selling digital copies of games through their own marketplace. We propose to build a complete system with a database and application for the user using SQL and react native.

The application will be similar to Steam or Google Play Store, users will be able to browse through our game collections, and then they will also be able to purchase and download if it is to their liking.

Our application will be specialized for mobile devices, and we will implement it using React Native as our front-end, NestJS as our back-end (REST API), and PostgreSQL as our database

You may peek at the design here: Figma Prototype

Target User

Our main audience will be mobile users who enjoy games
